Benifits of being bald.
Written by Alan Dsilva
Hair loss and going bald is one of the most terrifying worries that some men have. The strands of hair found on the pillow and the small patches of hair that continue to fall out can be a source of anxiety for many men. But the loss of hair and going bald doesn't need to be that bad. It turns out that baldness has its own advantages. So instead of focusing on the negatives, educate yourselves about the pros of going bald.
  • You have a lower risk of cancer
  • You have a more efficient metabolism
  • You seem more masculine
  • You save time and money blah blah blah...
